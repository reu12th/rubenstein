Hi! I'm Reuben Abdul, a results-driven IT professional and Systems Administrator with a strong foundation in Linux/Windows administration, hardware troubleshooting, and network operations. I am currently advancing my technical expertise through an HND in Networking and Cloud Computing at Yaba College of Technology.

I specialize in diagnosing complex OS failures, deploying secure technical solutions, and managing critical data systems. My experience ranges from executing live partition resizing using GParted and native Linux drivers, to repairing corrupted Windows bootloaders (fixmbr/fixboot) via command-line tools. I have a proven track record of deploying Linux Live USB environments to bypass Secure Boot restrictions and recover critical data from failing storage sectors.

Beyond OS and hardware infrastructure, I actively develop custom automation tools using Go and Python, and manage enterprise mobile device environments (MDM profile removal, global firmware flashing). With over 5 years of professional experience in technical documentation and user access management, I bridge the gap between technical execution and clear, cross-platform communication.
