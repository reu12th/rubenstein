Hi! I'm Reuben Abdul, a learner in the ALX Software Engineering Program and a Computer Science student at Yabatech. I currently work as an HMO Officer at Platinum Dental Surgery, a leading dental clinic in Lagos. My unwavering passion for technology and problem-solving has inspired me to aspire to become a skilled software engineer and make a notable impact in the tech industry.

Driven by a deep sense of purpose, my heart lies in building innovative solutions that positively impact people's lives. During my time at Platinum Dental Surgery, I undertook a remarkable data migration project, successfully moving data entry and clinical records to Google Cloud storage. This transformation allowed for easy access to critical information from anywhere and on any device, enhancing the clinic's efficiency and service quality.

With a relentless pursuit of knowledge and a commitment to continuous improvement, I am determined to stay at the forefront of technological advancements. I aim to leverage my skills (including HTML, CSS, Javascript, Git, Scrum, and Agile methodologies) to make a significant impact in the tech industry. I aspire to contribute to cutting-edge software projects in the near future.

My dedication to problem-solving, innovative thinking, and being a people person makes me a valuable asset for any technical team.

As I progress in my studies and career, I'm excited about the endless possibilities that software engineering offers. I look forward to exploring new opportunities that allow me to fulfill my goals and aspirations in this dynamic field. I am eager to leave a lasting mark in the ever-evolving world of technology
